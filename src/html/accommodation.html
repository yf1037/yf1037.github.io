Residence Inn by Marriott, Mt. Olive

271 Continental Drive, Mt. Olive, New Jersey 07874

Drive:

From I-80, take exit 25 for US-206. Then take the exit toward Waterloo Vlg/ International Trade Ctr. Turn left onto Continental Dr. Residence Inn will be on your right.

From US-206 S, take the Mount Olive exit. Turn left onto Continental Dr. Residence Inn will be on your right.

Public Transit:

Take Morristown Line (MNE) or Montclair-Booton Line (BNTN) train from Hoboken to Mount Olive station.
