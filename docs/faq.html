Ferquently Asked Questions

  • Q: Where can I find the wedding pictures and videos?

    You can find all the professional photos and videos here. The password is the wedding date in MMDDYYYY format. Thank you for celebrating with us!

  • Q: How can I RSVP?

    You can RSVP by returning the RSVP card provided in the invitation or using the online RSVP. Your prompt response is greatly appreciated to assist us in meticulous wedding planning. Don't wait until the last day!

  • Q: Can I bring kids or significant others?

    We are delighted to welcome children to our celebration. However, in an effort to ensure an intimate and meaningful gathering, we kindly request you to refrain from bringing a significant other unless the relationship has been established for a significant period. We look forward to sharing this joyous occasion with our nearest and dearest.

  • Q: Do you have a registry?

    Yes! We do have a registry, and we would be grateful if you could check it to prevent duplication. If your selected gift does not align with our registry, we kindly request cash contributions to address our specific needs. Your thoughtful understanding and support mean a great deal to us.

  • Q: What is the dress code?

    Semi-formal. Dress for pictures and comfort! The venue has a very scenic garden and there will be a photo booth.

  • Q: How can I get there?

    Driving is the most convienent and parking is free, but the hotel is also assessible from public transit (details on the Accomodation page). If you are considering carpooling, please provide us with your phone number to join the group chat. Additionally, please make sure to let us know in the RSVP if you need shuttle to and from the hotel.

  • Q: Do you have a hashtag for social media posting?

    Yes! Please add #SAYlove2024 to your social media posts.

  • Q: Is the ceremony indoor or outdoor?

    The ceremony will be held outdoors if weather permits and indoors in the event of rain.

  • Q: Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

    Yes! But we kindly ask you to mute your phone.

  • Q: Can you accomodate special diet?

    Yes. Please let us know in your RSVP. We can provide gluten-free, lactose-free, vegetarian, and vegan diet. And please let us know if you have any food allergies.

  • Q: Do you have a guestbook?

    Yes! Please take a picture at the photo booth and sign the scrap book.
